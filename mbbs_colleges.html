Get Direct Admission In Top MBBS Medical Colleges In Bangalore - Chennai - Pondicherry And All Major Cities

MBBS is one of the most desired and respected professions in India. Almost every year many students give entrance exams for MBBS, but only a few get the chance to study in those prestigious medical colleges. Earlier, in India, there were many different entrance exams conducted for medical entrance.

AIPMT was the national level exam and its scorecard was accepted in every medical college, whereas state level exams were also conducted as well as some of the private colleges conducted its own exam. But, in 2016 Supreme Court Of India changed the whole procedure of entrance exam and collaborated into a single exam called NEET-UG and NEET-PG. Since all the other exams are debarred now, competition has become more stiff and tough.

According to the latest statistics, almost seven lakh students appeared for the exam, and approximately around three to four lakh students qualified for 31,000 seats in the country. The success rate in these exams is very, low which goes up to four percent. Many students even after qualifying, lack in scores and ranks due to which they are not able to fulfill their dreams of becoming a doctor. But, here, we are giving an opportunity, to all the students to get direct admission to one of the top medical colleges of India. We are providing direct admission in MBBS, through management seats/NRI quota/management quota.

About MBBS (Bachelor Of Medicine And Bachelor Of Surgery)

MBBS is a graduate degree that is of almost five years, in which four years are for studying and one year for internship. The course starts with some of the basic clinical subjects such as biochemistry, physiology, anatomy, microbiology, pathology, and pharmacology. In a year internship, students are switched to work from one specialty to another and apart from these, one also gets experience in clinical care, ward management, staff management, and counseling skills. The course has been structured in such a way, that, students get up to date with what they are dealing with. MBBS is a profession where you can make changes to someone’s life. If you are looking for such a profession then, this is the right place to get direct admission to MBBS.

After completing internship students go for postgraduate studies, so that, students can specialize in one particular subject of their interests. Before, going for postgraduate studies, students in their internship get exposure to different specialties, so that, they can make a well-informed decision and work in the subject of their interest. As a student completes his/her post-graduation they become specialized in one subject with a degree of MD.

ELIGIBILITY FOR MBBS

There are criteria that have to be fulfilled by every student to get direct admission in MBBS. These criteria are eligible to get direct admission in one of the top medical institutes of India. They are-

  • The students must have passed ‘10+2’ with a matriculation degree in biology, physics, chemistry, and English.
  • For general category students, marks scored in biology, physics, chemistry should be 50% or above, and for reserved it should be 40% or above.
  • Candidate must be NEET Qualified.

These are the requirements that are essential for every student who wants to pursue MBBS. For further details on direct admission in MBBS, you may contact us and we will help with the admission process.
