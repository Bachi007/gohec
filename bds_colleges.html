BDS ( Bachelor Of Dental Surgery )

BDS is a graduate degree of five years duration including a one-year internship. This profession is slowly growing in demand as oral health has become an important aspect of everyone’s life. Entrance for BDS requires the students to have the following qualities like patience, concentration, accuracy, a methodical and a scientific approach to deal with the subjects and profession. The profession involves the treatment of teeth, oral health, gum, and other parts of the oral cavity. If you have such skills then you are considering the right field for yourself. So, don’t sit back and wait for the opportunity to come. Apply for BDS admissions 2017.

 

Why To Take Admission (Direct or Management Quota) in BDS / Dental Colleges?

 

With emerging technology, this field has been changing rapidly and thus creating more job opportunities and career prospects. With the introduction of new fields such as orthodontics, periodontics, oral pathology there are many job opportunities and career prospects available for BDS graduates. In BDS there is a new emerging field called cosmetic surgery and is in very much demand. This particular surgery is for cosmetic correction of your teeth. This course in BDS pays very well to the dental surgeons. Some of the other job opportunities or career prospects for BDS graduates are:-

 

  • Government Sector– There are many government hospitals which hire dental surgeons and they get fairly good payscales and incentives. Dental Surgeons are hired in many departments like health department, Dental clinic and nursing homes.
  • Private Sector- Many private hospitals hire BDS students with good salary package and incentives. In many private companies BDS graduate are hired as medical consultant.
  • Research and Higher Studies– After graduation you can go for further studies like post graduation and becoming specialised in any subject of your interest or you can go for research in dental or oral health. With research you can contribute in the evolvement of this field.
  • Self-Employment– This choice also pay you very well in your career. Be your own boss and work as a practitioner.

 

ELIGIBILITY FOR ADMISSION IN BDS / Dental COLLEGES

 

There are some criterias to be fulfilled by the students for getting admission in BDS 2017 and they are-

  • Must have matriculation in biology, physics, chemistry and english from a recognised board like ICSE, CBSE or any other state board.
  • Must have 50% or above aggregate in ‘10+2’ class.
  • Must have scored good marks or qualified NEET-UG.
