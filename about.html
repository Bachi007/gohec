About GoHEC


GoHEC Education is a leading and well-known Education Consultancy Firm based in Bangalore, Chennai, Pondicherry, Trivandrum, Raipur, Delhi, Ahmadabad, Mumbai, Pune & Jaipur with an expert in career counseling for national and international educational institutions. We provide assistance to students for admissions in various educational institutions including Government Universities, Deemed Universities, and Private institutes in India and Abroad. GoHEC Educational Consultancy has its representatives and centers all over India and together we help students find the right college which matches their aspirations and budgets.

Since the past 12 years (2008), GoHEC Educational Consultancy has assisted thousands of students in securing admissions in the field of Information Technology, Management, Engineering, Traditional & Vocational programs. We offer admission to the best universities and colleges affiliated with National and State Government Agencies such as AICTE, UGC, BCI, PCI, DCI, and NCTE.

GoHEC Education provides right guidance and admission to eligible students in all courses including M.Phil./PhD, B.Tech/M.Tech, BA/BSc/BCom, MA/MSc/MCom, LLB/LLM, B.Ed./MEd, B.Pharma/M.Pharma, BBA/MBA, BCA/MCA, DMLT/BSc MLT, and other diploma courses covering the entire range of Regular, Online Courses as well as Distance Education.

Our Vision
