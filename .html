About B Aeronautical (Bachelor’s In Aeronautical Engineering)

B Aeronautical Engineering is the sub branch of aerospace engineering. It is the study of aviation technology and its sub branches. It was the invention of aeroplane by Wright Brothers that let this field to expand and it got included in universities as branch to study. Aeronautical Engineering is a four year program included internship with a project in last year. As technology has expanded, aeronautical engineering has expand itself in terms of knowledge, jobs and skill. Some of the eminent Indian personalities in Aeronautical Science are-
  • APJ Abdul Kalam (President of India).
  • Kalpana Chawla (First Indian Woman to reach into space).
  • Dr. K Radhakrishnan (Chairman Of ISRO during 2009-2014).
  • Dr. Satish Dhawan (Father Of fluid dynamic research in India and Chairman of ISRO during 1972).
  • Dr. Vikram Sarabhai (Pioneer of space programs in India).
